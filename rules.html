Rules:



By using Mercatio, you, the user, must agree to the following terms before continuing. If some trade violates any of these rules, we maintain the right to remove the trade.


1. Every item that is a "Have", or an item that you are willing to trade, you must already own, and can not be stolen, borrowed, or rented.

2. When a trade is initiated between two users, both users have to have the items at the time of trade, and the trade must take place less than 72 hours after agreed to.

3. All items being traded, and being set up for trade, must be school appropriate, if you wouldn't give it to a teacher, you can't give it to a student.

4. Photos submitted must be of actual trade item.

5. All trades can never take place during school hours. They can happen before or after school hours, never during.

6. No actual currency can be traded for an item.

7. Once a trade is completed, consider it final, meaning no "trade-backs".

8. If an item is listed and not traded within 30 days of postage, the T.C.O, Nicholas and Robert, will take the item down.
