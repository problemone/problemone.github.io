Transparent. Honest. Fair. Fun.

Trading among your classmates has never been easier.
Mercatio was created by Nicholas and Robert, high school students at Khan Lab School in Mountain View, California. The project began with discussions on how society puts a value on items, which lead to conversations regarding currency. This got them thinking about what else can function/has functioned as money. The interaction between a public market and the value that people put on their own items fascinated them. Mercatio was developed from the ground up with the desire to create a student market, free of real world currency, where a person's percieved value interacts directly with the outcomes. Put simply, it is a student led market where items can be recycled, reused, and re-loved.
