Mercatio was created by Nicholas and Robert, high school students at Khan Lab School in Mountain View, California. The project began with discussions on how society puts a value on items, which lead to conversations regarding currency. This got them thinking about what else can function/has functioned as money. The interaction between a public market and the value that people put on their own items fascinated them.
Mercatio was developed from the ground up with the desire to create a student market, free of real world currency, where a person's perceived value directly affects the outcome. Put simply, it is a place where you can recycle, reuse, and re-love other people's items. Trading among your classmates has never been easier.
