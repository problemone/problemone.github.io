FAQ:



Q: What is the largest photo size that I can post?

A: We are limited to 10 MB, otherwise, your photo might not show up on the items page.


Q: The person that I wanted to trade with didn't go through with it, what can I do?

A: You can put the item and request back onto the page, allowing for other users to view it.


Q: What if my item is broken/not working, could I trade it?

A: Yes, but you have to disclose it in the title or description, otherwise it may be taken down.


Q: How many items can I trade in total?

A: At the moment, as many as you want, but repeats will be taken down.


Q: Are there re-trades for items?

A: Yes, but it has to be within 72 hours of the original trade.


Q: What if I don't know what I want to trade or what I want from a trade?

A: The trader must put, in the description of the missing item, an overall category of what they want, photos are not mandatory.


Q: What happens if multiple people want my item?

A: The entire system is based on first come first serve, so the first person who contacted you should be the person to follow through.


Q: If I really want an item, could I use money to buy it?

A: No, Mercatio should remain free of real world currency.


Q: How many days should it take to trade an item?

A: Once the trade is established, it should happen within 72 hours of agreement.


Q: How long does it take for my trade to be live?

A: It should be instantanious, if not, don't be afraid to contact us.


Q: When can we trade among other schools?

A: Mercatio is always growing, we will update everyone when these transactions are possible.
